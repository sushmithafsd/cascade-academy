Adavanced Laboratory

At Cascade Academy of Arts and Science College, our laboratories are equipped to provide an immersive and practical learning environment across multiple scientific disciplines.

The Microbiology Laboratory offers a sterile, highly controlled space where students explore microbial life through hands-on experiments such as microbial isolation, staining, antibiotic sensitivity testing, and food and water microbiology. It features a dedicated culture room, Class II laminar flow hoods, advanced microscopes, autoclaves, BOD incubators, and spectrophotometers, supporting in-depth research using diverse bacterial and fungal strains.

The Electronics and Communication Laboratory empowers students with the tools to design, analyze, and test modern communication systems and embedded technologies. Equipped with analog and digital trainer kits, oscilloscopes, microprocessor and Arduino development boards, AM/FM modulators, fiber optics systems, soldering stations, and LabVIEW software, this lab provides essential training in circuit design, signal processing, IoT applications, and project development.

Meanwhile, the Biotechnology Laboratory serves as a hub for advanced molecular biology and genetic engineering practices. It includes PCR machines, gel electrophoresis units, refrigerated centrifuges, spectrophotometers, biosafety cabinets, CO₂ incubators, and a dedicated tissue culture area, enabling experiments in DNA amplification, cloning, enzyme assays, and diagnostics.

Additionally, our General Science Laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology are fully furnished with modern benches, lab safety systems, and hands-on experimental setups for mechanics, optics, titration, thermodynamics, synthesis, and biological dissection. All labs are supported by qualified instructors and adhere to rigorous safety protocols, ensuring students gain the technical competence and confidence needed for academic and professional success
